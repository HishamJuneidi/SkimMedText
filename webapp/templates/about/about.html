About

Reading medical paper can be extremely time consuming and takes a lot of effort. Regular people and even Researchers find it quite challenging to understand the terminology used in research medical papers or even skim through it with decent understanding. This dilemma motivate SkimPaper project to help translate complex text to more readable word by summarizing a challenging to read medical paper to more simpler text. Also, the project tend to categorize the original text to

Background, Objectives, Methods, Results, and conclusions.

This will make absorbing any medical record or research paper more faster and efficient.
